What is expected of a Member of WGCOP?

The working group welcomes all ymca members. The number of accreditations given to the ymca has been decided by the UN to a limited amount. We therefore require specific commitments from members who aspire to join the wgcop as a member of the inside group. This commitment is set in relation to the period of the working group on COP21 from august 2015 until february 2016 and hopefully onward for the COP22-period ending in february 2017.
