Costs for an individual participating in Paris

These are the things you are required to pay for yourself (or through other means, e.g. your national YMCA) in order to go to Paris.

  • Travel to and from Paris.
  • Stay at Camp Climate (includes breakfast) - 120per week.
  • All meals except breakfast for the time you're staying in Paris.
  • Participation in COY - 25
