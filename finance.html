Financing, Accreditations & Age requirements:

The members of the Working Group will work together to acquire partial funding for participants, but we urge members to acquire substantial funds through their national movements, private sources or otherwise. Please also see the summary of the costs involved for you as an individual to participate in Paris.

Accreditations will be given out regarding a number of criteria, including age, gender and global south/north balance as well as individual dedication, experience etc. In order to participate in the conference, we require you to be 18 years of age (or older). Younger members are able to join the WGCOP and participate in the Outside Group and Camp Climate, as long as they're traveling with an adult (someone with an age >18).
