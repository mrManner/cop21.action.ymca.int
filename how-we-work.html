Working through the online platform of the RGE

The actual work in the working group is mostly done through the Internet, with meetings every two weeks where we develop strategies and plan together. When scheduling meetings, the working group will try itâ€™s very best to accommodate participants from various time-zones. Prior to the meetings an agenda will be sent out to for the participants to view and add comments. Between meetings, group-members will have assigned tasks to work on. Updates on the various tasks or assignments will then be presented by members or coalitions of group-members at each meeting. New discussions will be held to reach decisions on how to further the groups work. The working group is a member-driven process and focuses on what is considered important for the YMCA-RGE and the ultimate goal of COP21, in collaboration with other working groups.
