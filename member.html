Becoming a Member of WGCOP

As a member of the Working Group on COP you will collaborate with youth from all continents as an integral part of shaping the climate and environmental agenda of the World YMCA ahead of the decisive meeting in Paris. The WGCOP is dependant on its members and you will have a unique opportunity to help shape the Working Group’s agenda. You will also have the opportunity to:

  • Become a member of the YMCA-RGE WGCOP and its Paris delegation
    • either through an accreditation to COP21 in Paris as a member of the ‘Inside group’
      • an accreditation as a part of the Activism, Communications or Policy Group
        • become an integral part of our WG-Teams in Finance, Activism, Communications and Policy
    • or a spot as a member of the ‘Outside group’ working together with the inside team in Paris.
      • a chance to take part in one of the most important civil society mobilization in history by engaging with:
        • YMCA - Camp Climate
        • The Inside Team
        • Massive Civil Society Mobilization & Demnstrations
        • Other Youth Organisations

(most WGCOP members will most likely be a part of both the inside and the outside team)

The Working Group will also examine the possibility of attending intersessionals and / or having a gathering through the greater organisation of the YMCA-RGE.
